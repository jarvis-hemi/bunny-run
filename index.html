🐰 Bunny Run!
A Forest Adventure
Guide the bunny through the forest, collecting carrots and berries!

📖 How to Play
🥕 Carrots — 10 points each
🍓 Berries — 25 points each
🍄 Mushrooms — Power up! Chase predators for bonus points!

Predators have different behaviors:
🦊 Fox — Directly chases you
🦅 Hawk — Swoops in bursts
🐍 Snake — Ambushes from sides
🦉 Owl — Circles and swoops
